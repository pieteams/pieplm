# Chinese PLM Consulting & Implementation Company List

This document systematically compiles **common Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) consulting, implementation, and platform providers in the Chinese market**, covering the complete capability spectrum from **strategic planning, governance design, platform construction to implementation delivery**.

> Reference sources: e-works Digital Enterprise Network, CCID Consulting, IDC China, vendor official websites (as of 2025)

> Target audience:
>
> * Enterprise clients conducting PLM project selection and RFP preparation
> * Consulting/solution personnel conducting market and vendor capability analysis
> * PLM implementation, product data management, and digital transformation projects
> * Localization substitution and Xinchuang (信创) project selection

---

## 1. Vendor Classification

For easier understanding and selection, vendors are categorized by **core roles in PLM projects**:

* **Category A: Consulting + Strategic Planning**
  Focused on PLM top-level design, product innovation process planning, standards and system design.

* **Category B: Local Implementation & Platform Delivery**
  Focused on PLM system construction, implementation delivery, and platform landing — typically the main contractor.

* **Category C: Platform Provider + Data Governance Support**
  Provides mature PLM/data governance platforms, typically delivered through ecosystem partners.

> In practice, the common combination is: **A (consulting) + B (implementation) + C (platform)**.

| Scenario | Recommended Direction |
|----------|----------------------|
| PLM top-level design / consulting planning | CCID Consulting / iSoftStone |
| PLM system implementation (international vendors) | HAND / Inossem |

> In practice, the **"consulting + implementation + platform" combination model** is typically adopted to reduce project risk.

> Under the localization trend, **domestic PLM + domestic CAD + Xinchuang adaptation** is an important selection direction.

## 6. Market Trends (2024-2025)

1. **Localization acceleration** — Driven by Xinchuang policy, more enterprises are turning to domestic PLM
2. **Cloud/SaaS transformation** — Kingdee, Yonyou, KMSoft and others launching cloud PLM solutions
3. **AI+PLM convergence** — Large models empowering design collaboration, knowledge management, process planning
4. **Industry verticalization** — Deepening in aerospace, automotive, equipment and other segments
5. **Deep integration with domestic CAD** — Hoteamsoft, CAXA, ZWSOFT bridging design-management chains
